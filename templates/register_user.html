Disaster Management Act Information

Prior to the enactment of the Disaster Management Act, 2005, the Tamil Nadu Government in its order No. 323, Revenue (NC-11(1)) Department dated 08-07-2003 issued orders for the constitution of State Disaster Management.

The Disaster Management Act, 2005, provides for a systematic and planned approach to disaster preparedness and response at all levels of government. The act defines the roles of the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA), State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA), and District Disaster Management Authority (DDMA) in ensuring effective coordination and response during disasters.

As per the act, the government ensures immediate relief, rescue operations, and long-term rehabilitation support to affected populations. Various schemes such as the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) and National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) are implemented to provide financial aid and infrastructure rebuilding.
